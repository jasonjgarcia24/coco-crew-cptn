# Fueling Baseline

**Source: trained values + sweat rate testing.**

## Hourly targets (FLOOR — never below)
| Metric | Day / Moderate | Day / High Effort + Hot | Night / Cold |
|--------|---------------|-------------------------|--------------|
| **Calories** | ≥ 240 kcal/hr | ≥ 240 kcal/hr | ≥ 240 kcal/hr |
| **Sodium** | ≥ 500 mg/hr | ≥ 500 mg/hr | ≥ 500 mg/hr |
| **Fluid** | 1.0 L/hr | 1.25 L/hr | 0.5 L/hr |

## Sweat rate baseline
| Period | Sweat rate | Sodium loss |
|--------|-----------|-------------|
| Day | 1.0 L/hr | 757 mg/L (≈ 757 mg/hr) |
| Night | 0.5 L/hr | 757 mg/L (≈ 378 mg/hr) |

⚠️ **Sodium intake (500 mg/hr) is in slight DAY deficit (-257 mg/hr).** Acceptable for short stretches, but watch for hyponatremia indicators **and** dehydration indicators in the hottest sections (Cottonwood→Crown King, Mingus→Jerome descent into Verde Valley). When effort is high + heat extreme, **bias toward 700+ mg/hr via Liquid IV or extra capsules.**

## Fueling floor (contract Section III)
- **Calories per hour, never below: 240 kcal/hr**
- **Sodium per hour, never below: 500 mg/hr** (push to 700+ mg/hr in extreme heat)
- **Fluid per hour minimums:** see table above
- "Eat even if nauseous" through Crown King M36.6

## Heat ceiling (contract Section IV)
- **Heart rate ceiling in heat sections: 165 bpm**
- HR should mostly remain MODERATE for this distance — 165 is the hard ceiling, not the target
- IF perceived effort spikes → walk, ice, drink, restart only after pulse drops below ceiling

## Default fueling cadence (set timer)
**Every 20 min on the trail:** sip + chew/gel
- 1 personal mix flask (50g 2:1 Malto:Fructose + 1× LiquidIV) carries **248 cal + 510 mg sodium + 380 mg potassium** = **1 hour of base fueling**
- Top off carb deficit with a Neversecond every 60-90 min if appetite allows
- Caffeine via Neversecond (75 mg) — see caffeine section below

## At every AS (drop bag or pass-through)
1. **Sip warm liquid first** (broth / coffee / hot mix) — opens GI
2. **Solid food** (real food preferred at AS — pizza, quesadilla, fruit)
3. **Refill on-person fueling** — flasks back to 1L day / 0.5L night
4. **Restock pocket fuel** — 2× Neversecond + salt caps
5. **Don't leave AS without a full belly + full flasks**

## Caffeine plan
**Source:** Neversecond gels (75 mg caffeine version) + AS coffee
**Cutoff before sleep:** 60 min (no caffeine within 60 min of planned sleep)
**Sleep stops to time around:** Camp Wamatochick (M67.4) → Sedona Posse (M159.1) → Fort Tuthill (M211.0)

| Phase | Window | Cumulative caffeine (mg) | Notes |
|-------|--------|-------------------------|-------|
| Pre-race | Sun + Mon AM | minimal | Don't pre-dose race-day; heat amplifies vasodilation. |
| Day 1 (Mon) | 5 AM → 8 PM | 0–75 mg | Low/none through Cottonwood→CK heat. Optional Neversecond w/ caffeine after CK. |
| Night 1 (Mon→Tue, post-CK) | 8 PM → ~5 AM | 75–150 mg | After CK, can start. Stop 60 min before Camp Wamatochick sleep. |
| Day 2 (Tue) — long push to Sedona Posse | 6 AM → 8 PM | 150–225 mg | This is the long awake stretch (~18-23h FAST/A-GOAL). Spread caffeine evenly. Stop 60 min before Sedona Posse arrival. |
| Night 2 (Tue→Wed, post-Sedona Posse) | 8 PM → ~5 AM | 75 mg | After 60-min Sedona Posse sleep. Stop 60 min before Fort Tuthill arrival. |
| Day 3 (Wed)+ — final push | from Fort Tuthill on | 75–150 mg | Final 42 mi push. Caffeine OK; no more planned sleeps after FT. |

⚠️ **Daily caffeine ceiling: 300 mg.** No more than 4× Neversecond-w-caffeine per 24h.

## Coconut water — placement strategy
Canned coconut water is your **post-heat-section recovery shot**. Place in drop bags AT the END of hot sections (so you chug on arrival, not during the hot stretch):
- **Crown King drop bag (M36.6)** — recovery from Cottonwood→CK heat
- **Mingus Mountain drop bag (M107.2)** — recovery from Watson→Fain→Mingus climb
- **Dead Horse drop bag (M132.9)** — recovery from Mingus→Jerome Verde Valley descent (hottest of mid-race)
- **Sedona Posse drop bag (M159.1)** — recovery from Sedona heat before Schnebly climb
- **Fort Tuthill drop bag (M211.0, via CK bag)** — recovery from Walnut→Fort Tuthill push

## Personal meal replacement — placement
**443 kcal + 800 mg sodium = 1.8 hrs of fueling.** Use at sleep stops to load carbs + sodium without volume:
- **Camp Wamatochick (M67.4)** — 1 meal pre-sleep
- **Mingus Mountain (M107.2)** — 1 meal pre-sleep
- **Sedona Posse (M159.1)** — 1 meal pre-sleep
- **Fort Tuthill (M211.0)** — 1 meal pre-sleep

## Nausea protocol
See `03-failure-cards/gi-distress.md` re-feeding ladder.
- IF can't tolerate personal mix → switch to Neversecond + plain water
- IF can't tolerate gels → coconut water + AS solid food (small bites)
- IF severe → walk it off, then 50% strength mix, ramp back up
