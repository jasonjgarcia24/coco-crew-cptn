## How to use this table at every AS
1. **Look at "actual TOD" vs. the FAST/A-GOAL window.** Are you inside it?
2. **If between FAST and A-GOAL** → on plan; execute as designed
3. **If between A-GOAL and B-GOAL** → behind plan; minimize dwell, skip optional sleep, no foot care unless critical
4. **If past B-GOAL** → at DQ risk; tell medic, evaluate DNF/DNFing options at next AS, **CRITICAL: rebuild buffer or pull**
5. **If approaching cutoff** → reassess every AS
