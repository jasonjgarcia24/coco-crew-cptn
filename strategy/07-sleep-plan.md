## Sleep ritual (rehearse)
1. **Tell AS volunteer your wake time** (verify AS staff can wake you if alarm fails)
2. **Eat first** — 1 personal meal replacement (50g carb, 800mg sodium, 443 cal). You won't want to eat after.
3. **Foot care if scheduled** (see atlas pages for which sleep stops include foot care)
4. **Set TWO alarms:** phone + watch
5. **Layer up** (Camp Wamatochick + Sedona Posse + Fort Tuthill all indoor; OK to undress slightly)
6. **Eye mask + ear plugs** if available
7. **Lie feet-up** if possible — drains lower-leg edema
8. **Wake → Neversecond w/ caffeine OR coffee → 1 personal mix flask refilled → walk out**

## Caffeine timing rule (cross-ref `05-fueling-baseline.md`)
**No caffeine within 60 min of planned sleep window.**
- Stop caffeine ≥1 hr before:
  - Camp Wamatochick arrival
  - Sedona Posse arrival
  - Fort Tuthill arrival

If caffeine timing was missed, sleep is compromised — accept shorter / lower-quality nap.

## Trail-side micro-naps (emergency only)
If hours from a sleep station and cooked, see `03-failure-cards/sleep-monsters.md`:
- Sheltered, off-trail spot 10+ ft from course
- Bivy or trash bag
- 15–20 min alarm (NOT longer — you'll get colder)
- Caffeine before lying down (kicks in as you wake)

## Solo + crewless reminder
**Nobody is here to wake you. Two alarms. Always.**
AS volunteers may help but the responsibility is yours.
