## ⚠️ NO-PASSING ZONE
**Course location:** **Dead Horse (M132.5) → ~M136.5 (Lower Raptor trail through Arizona Primrose habitat).** Zone ENDS when you turn LEFT onto Lime Kiln Trail. ~4 miles total.

**Rules in the zone:**
- NO PASSING other runners
- NO LEAVING TRAIL (off-trail movement = DQ; protects Arizona Primrose, an endangered plant)
- Single-file
- Patience

If you arrive in the zone behind someone slower than you, that's the race plan now.

## ⚠️ NO-PACERS sections (Mexican Spotted Owl protection)
**Two course sections prohibit pacers entirely** (and require headlamps low + quiet):
- **Schnebly Hill (M176.1) → Munds Park (M190.0)** — owl nesting habitat
- **Wildcat Hill (M234.1) → Finish (M253.3)** — Mt Elden mating pairs, hatchlings emerging early May

**N/A for solo runner** — but it means you're alone in these sections regardless. Other crewed runners cannot pass off pacers either, so the trail will be sparser.

## What if you get lost
1. STOP. Don't make it worse.
2. Backtrack to last confirmed marker
3. SPOT Tracker = staff knows your position
4. If stuck, press SOS on SPOT (only if true emergency)
5. If 30 min lost, expect a sweeper or staff response
