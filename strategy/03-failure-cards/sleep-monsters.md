# 🌙 SLEEP MONSTERS

**Print + laminate. Copy in every drop bag from Mingus onward.**

## What this is
Sleep deprivation past ~30 hrs starts producing hallucinations, micro-sleeps, slowed reactions. By race night 2 (Tue→Wed) and especially night 3 (Wed→Thu), the monsters are real.

## Decision tree (at any moment on the trail)
- **IF falling asleep on feet** → STOP, sit, 5-min eyes-closed reset → if still drowsy → next sleep station, sleep 20+ min
- **IF mild visual hallucinations** (rocks look like animals) → caffeine + cold water on face + brisk walk → re-evaluate in 30 min
- **IF severe hallucinations** (people on trail who aren't there, hearing voices) → next sleep station, **mandatory 1 hr+ sleep**
- **IF can't navigate / can't recognize trail markers** → STOP, sit, eat, hydrate, 20-min nap on trail (set alarm) → reassess

## At sleep stations
**Pre-committed sleep windows (per contract):**
- **Camp Wamatochick (M67.4) — 30 min** (FIRST sleep, post-100k, indoor 🚿)
- **Sedona Posse Grounds (M159.1) — 60 min** (pre-Schnebly climb, biggest consolidated sleep, indoor)
- **Fort Tuthill (M211.0) — 30 min** (pre-final-42mi push, indoor, CK drop bag arrives)
- **TOTAL: 2.0 hours.** Emergency-sleep override applies if sleep monsters hit between scheduled stops.

**Mingus Mountain (M107.2) is the natural emergency fallback** between Camp Wamatochick and Sedona Posse — it's indoor 🚿 + medic, sits at the natural crisis zone of ~24-30 hrs awake.

**Sleep ritual (well-rehearsed):**
1. Tell AS volunteer your wake time
2. Eat BEFORE sleep (you won't want to eat after)
3. Set TWO alarms (phone + watch)
4. Lie down feet-up if possible
5. Eye mask + ear plugs if available
6. Wake → caffeine → eat → walk out

## Caffeine plan
- **Daily ceiling: 300 mg / 24h** (max 4× Neversecond-with-caffeine)
- **Sources:** Neversecond gels (75 mg/gel) + AS coffee
- **Cutoff before sleep: ≥60 min** — no caffeine within 60 min of any of the 4 sleep stops
- **Pre-race / Day 1 (Mon 5 AM → 8 PM):** minimal — heat amplifies vasodilation
- **Night 1 onward (after Crown King M36.6):** ramp up; 75 mg every 2–3 hrs is typical

## What NOT to do
- Don't try to "push through" hallucinations
- Don't drink alcohol at AS (no, really)
- Don't ignore the drowsy → falling asleep → hit head sequence
- Don't skip a planned sleep just because you "feel okay"

## Trail-side micro-naps
If hours from a sleep station and you're cooked:
- Find sheltered, off-trail spot 10+ ft from course
- Bivy or trash bag
- Set alarm 15–20 min
- Eat caffeine before lying down (kicks in as you wake)
- Resume after — slow walk first 5 min

## Solo + crewless reminder
You have nobody to wake you. Two alarms. Always.
