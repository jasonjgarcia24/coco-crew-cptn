# 🤢 GI DISTRESS

**Print + laminate. Copy in every drop bag.**

## Triggers
- Vomiting, can't keep food down
- Diarrhea
- Severe nausea preventing fueling
- Stomach cramping after fueling

## Decision tree
- **IF vomiting once + recovering** → 30 min walk, sip water, restart slow fueling at half rate
- **IF vomiting >2x in 1 hr** → walk to next AS, full stop, ice + sips of clear fluid + salty broth
- **IF can't keep fluids down for 2+ hrs** → next medic AS, get assessed
- **IF can't keep fluids down for 4+ hrs** → contract Section I trigger → next medic AS, **DROP** (DNFing rule applies — can re-enter if recover)
- **IF vomiting blood** → contract Section I → DROP IMMEDIATELY at next AS

## Re-feeding ladder (after a vomit episode)
1. 5 min: sips of water
2. 15 min: salty broth (pickle juice, ramen broth)
3. 30 min: 50 ml ginger ale or coke
4. 60 min: 1 cracker / 50 cal of bland carb
5. 90 min: 100 cal/hr fueling, slowly back to 200+

## Pre-empt
- Don't go above 80–100 g/hr carb intake in heat sections
- Salt with every calorie source
- No new foods on race day — only what you've trained on
- Slow + steady > big meals at AS

## Common Cocodona-specific GI causes
- Heat-induced gastroparesis (Cottonwood→Crown King)
- Caffeine spike when over-fueled
- Switching from gel-only to solid food too fast at AS
- Cold water on hot stomach
