# 🎒 GEAR CHECK FAILURE

**Print + laminate. Copy in every drop bag from CK forward.**

## What this is
6 mandatory gear checks on Cocodona 250. **Failure = DQ.** This is preventable.

## Gear check stations
| AS | Mile | What's checked |
|----|------|----------------|
| Start (DCR) | 0 | 4L water capacity |
| Cottonwood Creek | 7.4 | Must leave w/ ≥4L water |
| Crown King | 36.6 | Required Gear + Cold Weather Gear |
| Fain Ranch | 96.5 | Required + Cold Weather (IF leaving >10am) |
| Sedona Posse | 159.1 | Required + Cold Weather |
| Munds Park | 190.0 | Required + Cold Weather |
| Fort Tuthill | 211.0 | Required + Cold Weather |
| Wildcat Hill | 234.1 | Required + Cold Weather |

## Required Gear (carry ENTIRE course — per Aravaipa Runner Guide)
- **Cell Phone**
- **GPS Enabled Device** w/ course file loaded (smartphone in airplane mode recommended) + extra batteries / charging method
- **Headlamp** + extra batteries / charging method
- **Collapsible Cup** (cupless event for cold drinks — bring your own flask/cup; AS provides cups for hot drinks only)
- **Space Blanket**
- **Whistle**

## Cold Weather Gear (additional — required at gear check AS + cold sections)
- **4L water capacity** (only required leaving Start; 4L MIN leaving Cottonwood)
- **Gloves**
- **Warm Hat** (neck gaiter counts)
- **Long Sleeve**
- **Insulatory Upper Layer** — fleece, puffy, or mid-weight w/ insulation/thickness
- **Waterproof jacket w/ hood** — ONLY required if rain or snow forecast (check weather race-eve)

## Pre-AS-arrival checklist (every gear check AS)
1. 1 mi out: mental check — is everything I should have ON ME?
2. If anything's in pack: pull it out, put it accessible
3. At AS: AS volunteer asks → present gear cleanly
4. Don't argue — comply

## If you fail
- DQ — you're done. No appeal mid-race.
- The way to never fail: **gear stays on body, not in drop bag**

## Drop bag "swap, don't lose" rule
At every drop bag AS: if you swap a gear item (fresh puffy, new headlamp), the OLD item goes in the USED DROP BAG pile. **Don't leave the AS without the gear item AT ALL.**

## Cold sections (when Cold Weather Gear must be carried)
**From Crown King (M36.6) onward through the rest of the race**, you must carry Cold Weather Gear at all times. Confirmed cold zones:
- **Crown King → Kamp Kipa**: nighttime lows ~40°F (per Section Descriptions)
- **Mingus Mountain summit (~7,800 ft)**: 40°F day-to-night swing
- **Coconino Plateau (M176–253)**: avg nighttime lows upper-30s°F
- **Mount Elden climb (M234.1 → M249.4)**: lows ~25°F, wind chill below 0°F at altitude (~9,000'+)

Required Gear is on you the **entire course**. Cold Weather Gear specifically must be on you leaving every gear check AS.

## Daylight savings / time-of-day awareness
Fain Ranch: gear check applies if leaving after 10:00 AM. Don't get caught out by oversleeping a nap and triggering a check you didn't pack for.
