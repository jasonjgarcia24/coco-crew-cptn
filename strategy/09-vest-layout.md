# Per-leg loadout

## Start (DCR M0) → Cottonwood Creek (M7.4)
**~7.4 mi, +1,423' / -1,339'. Pre-dawn cool start. Sun rises ~5:30 AM, but heat doesn't bite until ~8:00–8:30 AM.**

**Pace target: 9:30–10 min/mi** for this section (faster than the model's average 10.75 min/mi). Goal: bank time before heat. Target arrival Cottonwood: ~6:10 AM Mon — ahead of FAST band by ~10 min.

**Calorie note:** **Don't worry about cal balance for this leg.** Cold dawn + banked breakfast carbs (oatmeal + banana ~3:30 AM) = you're not in deficit. Sip from the front mix flask to keep digestion online; coconut water is morale fuel; gels save for the climb.

## Calorie math (Cottonwood → Lane Mtn, where it matters)

**First leg (Start → Cottonwood)**: don't track. Cold + breakfast bank = no deficit.

**Second leg (Cottonwood → Lane Mtn, 25.1 mi)** — this is where calories matter:

| Metric | Carrying out of Cottonwood | FAST need (~7h) | A-GOAL need (~9h) | B-GOAL need (~13h) |
|--------|---------------------------|------------------|------------------|------------------|
| Calories | ~1,400–1,600 kcal* | 1,680 kcal | 2,160 kcal | 3,120 kcal |
| Sodium (heat 700–900 mg/hr) | ~5,500–6,500 mg | 4,900–6,300 | 6,300–8,100 | 9,100–11,700 |

*Estimate: ~190g residual carbs from start (4 unused gels + 1 partial mix flask) + 2 fresh LiquidIV mixes (24g) + 2 fresh Neversecond at Cottonwood (60g) + salty real food bite (~50g) ≈ 324g carb = ~1,300 kcal, plus salt-pill cal ≈ negligible.

**Conclusion:** FAST band is on target with the planned Cottonwood resupply. A-GOAL + B-GOAL bands need MORE at Cottonwood and MUST eat real food at Lane Mtn (medic AS). Lane Mtn is the second top-up — grab 2–3 more Neversecond + real food + 1 fresh LiquidIV mix.

⚠️ **Sodium is still the bigger risk than calories** — at 700+ mg/hr in extreme heat, the A-GOAL + B-GOAL bands run a deficit despite the plan. Watch for hyponatremia OR dehydration symptoms; bias toward salt-pill use and AS pretzels/chips/pickles whenever you stop.

## Refill discipline at every AS (after Crown King drop bag stop)
1. **Drink down old mix** to empty before refilling (or transfer to one consolidated flask)
2. **Refill water flasks** at AS water spigot
3. **Mix fresh personal mix** in 1 front + 1 back flask (powder + 1 LiquidIV per flask)
4. **Restock pocket** to 6 gels minimum
5. **Pop a salt cap** before leaving

## What NOT to do
- Don't leave Cottonwood with less than 4 L (gear check DQ)
- Don't ignore the empty side flasks (they're capacity, not optional)
- Don't switch to NEW carb sources mid-race (race what you trained on)
- Don't drink the coconut water past Cottonwood — it's a one-time start treat
- Don't pop caffeine pre-Crown King (heat amplifies vasodilation; caffeine OK after CK)
